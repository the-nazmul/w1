Bangladeshi journalist working at the intersection of data, investigations, and political economy.

Nazmul Ahasan is Executive Editor at Netra News , a public-interest journalism platform in Bangladesh.

Previously, he was a US economy reporter at Bloomberg News in Washington, DC, and earlier an editor at Radio Free Asia’s BenarNews, shaping coverage from South and Southeast Asia. Before moving to the United States, he worked at The Daily Star and Netra News in Dhaka.

He graduated from the UC Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism in 2023. His work has appeared in Foreign Policy , The Economist, The Telegraph , Haaretz , Columbia Journalism Review, and the San Francisco Chronicle .
